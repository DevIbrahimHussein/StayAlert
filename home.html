Our Aim

We are all prone to accidents, and at one point of our lives we've all experienced the utter panic of not knowing what do, or how to act when having one of our close ones in an accident whether critical or not, or in distress in general. So this website shall be your guide when you get stuck in a sticky situation, for it combines the most helpful emergency organizations in one place, that is StayAlert.
